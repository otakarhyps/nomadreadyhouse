Your Home in Europe's Surf Capital

Nomad Ready is more than a place to stay — it's a home designed for people who work remotely and live for the ocean. Nestled in the charming village of Ferrel, just minutes from the world-famous waves of Baleal and Supertubos, we offer private rooms with everything you need to be productive during the day and stoked after hours.

The house blends Scandinavian-inspired design with coastal warmth — think ergonomic workspaces meets surf culture, curated bookshelves meets film nights on the projector, and morning coffee on the sun terrace before your dawn patrol.

Work Ready

Herman Miller chair, height-adjustable desk, external monitor, laptop stand, and high-speed WiFi. Your most productive setup away from home.

Surf Ready

Board storage, wetsuit drying area, bikes to the beach, and 15-minute walk to Baleal. Supertubos and 20+ surf spots within a short drive.

Live Ready

Private room and bathroom, shared kitchen, film nights on the projector, sun terrace, and a vibrant community of like-minded nomads.

Where We Are

Ferrel, Peniche — Europe's Surf Capital

Ferrel is a charming Portuguese village where surfers blend in with local farmers and fishermen. It's a place that feels authentic, unhurried, and deeply connected to the ocean.

The Peniche coastline offers surfable waves in almost any conditions — from the sheltered beaches of Baleal to the world-class barrels of Supertubos, home to the WSL Championship Tour.

15 min walk to Baleal Beach
2 min walk to cafes & restaurants
~1 hour drive from Lisbon Airport

Local tip: Funky Donkey pizza is around the corner, the best chicken is next door, and plenty of fish restaurants are within walking distance.
